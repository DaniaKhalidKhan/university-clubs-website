Ushers And Events Management Society

Society Overview

The society works with university administration in organizing various events, including the events on the arrival of some guests inside or outside from the country, the events on the visits of the schools and colleges students and the orientation ceremony for new students.

Society Objectives

  • The objective of this society is to arrange and organize co-curricular and extra-curricular events in- and out-door of PAF-IAST.
  • The society also works with university administration in organizing various events, including the events on the arrival of some guests inside or outside from the country, the events on the visits of the schools and colleges students and the orientation ceremony for new students.
  • The society is very keen to promote PAF-IAST by arranging university trips, educational workshops, exhibitions, book fairs, and dinner nights for the students.

Functions of Society

  • This society is a team of experienced volunteers who will serve at all kinds of events irrespective of the departmental boundaries.
  • The Ushers are professionally trained as event organizers, as a result of their services at various events.
  • The society is also responsible to provide ushers for the events conducted by other clubs or societies to provide them with a vast exposure in event management.
