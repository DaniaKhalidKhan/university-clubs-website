Literary and Debating Society

Society Overview

Our mission is to provide opportunities for our members to explore the wider world of literature, as well as connecting them with students of other universities. Promote the literary and debating culture through professional’s lectures, Student Expos, debating competitions and knowledge workshops/Events etc.

Society Objectives

  • Promote the literary and debating culture through professional’s lectures, Student Expos, debating competitions and knowledge workshops/Events etc.
  • Providing a platform to enhance and update students public speaking skills with high level of management and self-confidence.
