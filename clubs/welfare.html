Welfare Society

Society Overview

The Welfare Society envisions a compassionate institute community where students actively contribute to social responsibility and uplift the less fortunate. Through volunteerism and impactful initiatives, we aim to foster empathy, equity, and sustainable positive change both within and beyond the campus.

Society Objectives

  • Encouraging Volunteerism and Social Outreach
  • Raising Awareness on Social Issues
  • Fostering Leadership and Personal Development
